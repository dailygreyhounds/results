Reading the Results Table
📊ROI
Return on Investment. A ROI of +50% means for every $1,500 staked, you returned $2,250 on average. Calculated as (total returns − total staked) ÷ total staked × 100.
📈Running P/L
Cumulative profit or loss from bet one to now. Green means you're ahead of where you started; red means behind. Resets to zero at the beginning of tracking.
🎯Place Rate
How often a selection finishes 1st, 2nd or 3rd. A high place rate with lower win rate indicates a consistent model that's worth watching for each-way opportunities.
📅Frequency
How many selections per day a model generates on average. A model at 0.32/day fires roughly every 3 days. Lower frequency models need more patience but can be higher conviction.
